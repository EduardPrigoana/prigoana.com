Ad Blocking

Install these two extensions:

uBlock Origin Lite | uBlock Origin

Go to their settings and turn on all filters.

Why?

Blocks ads on Spotify, YouTube, protects from malware, removes cookie pop-ups, and improves browsing speed.

Trust Issues?

They're open-source. Check their code: uBO Lite | uBlock Origin.

Music Piracy

Torrenting:

Install qBittorrent and search for torrents on 1337x. Always use a VPN such as Cloudflare WARP.

Click the magnet link, check what you're downloading, deselect unwanted files, and start the download. If it has no seeds, try a different torrent.

Downloading:

For Spotify playlists or songs, use Lucida. Paste your playlist link and download. For higher quality, use Tidal forwarding.
